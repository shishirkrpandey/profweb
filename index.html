Dr. Shishir Kumar Pandey

Assistant Professor (Physics), Department of General Sciences, BITS Pilani – Dubai Campus. Computational condensed matter physicist focused on structural, electronic, and magnetic properties of materials.

About

Develops and applies first-principles electronic structure methods, model Hamiltonians, renormalization group tools, Monte Carlo simulations, and machine learning to study correlated systems, semiconductors, and topological insulators.
